Isha's favorite literary works

I have always loved to read, write, and listen! Here are some of my favorites in the media today.

Podcasts!

I subscribe to over 20 different podcasts. I listen to them whenever I drive or workout.

Newsletters!

The second I wake up, I usually read 2-3 newsletters to kick start my day and catch up on what I missed.

Writing!

While I enjoy novels as much as the next guy, I love picking out short pieces such as poems or articles to always come back to.

Speeches!

As a former debater, I admire great speakers and speeches. They inspire me and push me to do better in my own speaking.
